Favorite Sites



edhrec.com

EDHRec is a websited dedicated to a specific gameplay format called Commander for the popular card game Magic: The Gathering.

I am frequently building new decks and thus use this site for recommendations and ideas.

You can also reference archidekt.com which is a deck building tool that helps you keep

track of your decks and what cards are in them, making it easier to swap out cards/upgrade them.




amazon.com

Another frequent online visit of mine. I am always on amazon looking for good deals on videogames, board games, workout equipment, etc.

I am such a sucker for amazon that I do have an amazon prime membership. Prime has honestly been a big help during the pandemic

however since it is a risk to leave the house and I can conveniently order the things I need from the comfort of my home and expect

them within just a couple of days.
